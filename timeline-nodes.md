## Pre-History

**1009.9 · HESPERON**
### enters the academy
Leaves the demon homelands for the human-fusion academic system. Fe recalibrates in a world where no one has horns.

**1010.9 · PHORION**
### enters university
Eighteen. Already knows how people work. Does not yet know what to do with it.

**1013.9 · HESPERON**
### Leon
A classmate who never flinches at sharp edges. For the first time, the filter can stay off. Not a match — a rest stop.

**1019.6 · HESPERON**
### PhD defense
The committee votes unanimously. He leaves the room before they finish announcing it. The only handwritten draft stays in his desk.

**1014.10 · PHORION**
### enters the system
Twenty-two, clean suit, no style yet. The smile already has two layers.

**1017.6 · PHORION**
### four pillars
Trust betrayed, debts weaponized, incompetence protected, loyalty sold. Four lessons, all defensive. The network begins to form.

**1020.6 · PHORION**
### political operative
No longer adapting to the system. Using it. The sharpness sits closer to the surface now. Tea habit acquired.

## War

**1020.2 · ×** ★
### war begins
The eastern front opens. A researcher is pulled from his postdoc. A political operator moves to the command car. Both lives reorder within the month.

**1021.3 · HESPERON**
### glass cannon
Seven seconds. No visual effects. The wall fractures along paths only he calculated. The recording officer forgets to write.

**1022.9 · ×** ★
### first contact
A meeting P arranged to not look arranged. Six steps across the room. Not attraction — recognition.

**1022.10 · ×**
### the record shop
No agenda, no others, no rules of engagement. He talks about music longer than he meant to. The door opens a crack. P notices.

**1023.4 · PHORION**
### M recruited
An expelled student's anonymous post happens to detonate near P's political enemy. She signs a contract without reading the fine print. Fifty furious messages follow. A tradition honored.

**1024.10 · HESPERON**
### facility incident
Two fractured ribs, pleural effusion, magic depletion. He tells the rescue team he is fine. Phorion finds out in twelve minutes, calls four people, saves the data first.

**1024.10 · ×** ★
### relationship confirmed
One night of arithmetic. Every model says the loss is survivable. His hands shake anyway. In the morning: two sentences, one conclusion, a hand held a fraction too long.

**1024.12 · PHORION**
### the accident
A precisely arranged collision targeting his usual seat. He moved because a camera angle changed. Seven stitches. Five seconds of hesitation before calling H.

## Post-War

**1025.4 · ×** ★
### war ends
P takes a quiet office. H gets a library desk and a lab with no deadlines. The policy that funds the lab has no traceable author.

**1024.12 · ×**
### first kiss
P's apartment, after dinner, on the sofa. He closes the distance and pauses. The last window. H does not step back.

**1026.1 · ×**
### shared apartment
A dormitory renovation that never ends. Two suitcases, a stack of books, and door frames adjusted for horns. The closet was already half empty.

**1027.6 · ×**
### now
One year of cohabitation. Curtains replaced, mattress upgraded, temperature calibrated to the half-degree. Neither has mentioned it.
